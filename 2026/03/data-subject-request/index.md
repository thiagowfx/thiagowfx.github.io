
Basic template to request **interview feedback**:

> **Subject**: Data Subject Request
>
> **To**: dpo@{company}.com[^1]
>
> {Greetings},
>
> I'd like to get access to my interview feedback ("personal information") from
> my last application at {company} as a candidate ({NN} {weeks,years} ago). My
> email is {email}.
>
> I am emailing this alias as per
> https://{company}.com/{privacy-terms}.
>
>
> {Goodbye}
> {Your name}

**Disclaimer**: This is not legal advice, I am not a lawyer.

In Europe the GDPR generally entitles candidates to receive a copy of their
data (e.g. verbatim interview notes) within 30 days.

You can also apply the same idea to request **performance reviews** and
**calibration notes** from your current or former employers.

I used this various times throughout the years in both scenarios. It is very
effective. YMMV.

[^1]: Keywords: data protection officer (DPO), data protection, candidate
    privacy, GDPR, DSVGO, SAR (Subject Access Request). Use your favorite search
    engine or your favorite LLM to find the appropriate point of contact or
    channel.
