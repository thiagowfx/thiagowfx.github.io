## Ingredients

In order, add to a glass:

- **coconut milk** (1/2 cup)
- **chia seeds** (2 tbsps)
- **maple syrup / honey** (sweetener)
- vanilla extract
- cinnamon
- a pinch of salt

## Preparation

Whisk everything.

Let it soak overnight in the fridge.

Top with blueberries, raspberries or cherries. Or with nuts.
