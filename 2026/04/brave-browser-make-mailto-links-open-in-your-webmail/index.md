
Stack Overflow: [How to make mailto: links to go to
Gmail?](https://stackoverflow.com/questions/71549037/how-to-make-mailto-links-to-go-to-gmail)

The described solution is platform agnostic — it should work for other email
providers than Gmail as well.

**TL;DR**: Visit `brave://settings/handlers`:

> Sites can handle special tasks when you click on certain types of links, like
> creating a new message in your email client or adding new events to your
> online calendar

...and then set your preferred webmail client as the **Default**.

If it does not show up on the list, visit its homepage first.
