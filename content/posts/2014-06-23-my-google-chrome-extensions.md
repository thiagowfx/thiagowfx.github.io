A snapshot of the Chrome extensions I currently use (not regularly updated).

## Metadata

- Chromium version: Chrome/35.0.1916.153
- Operating System: Arch Linux x86_64

## Extensions (24)

- **DuckDuckGo for Chrome** v42.5.17 — DuckDuckGo enhancements for Chrome
- **Vimium** v1.44 — Keyboard shortcuts for navigation (Vim-like)
- **Hacker Vision** v1.28 — High-contrast dark theme for all websites
- **IBA Opt-out** (by Google) v1.5 — Opt out of interest-based ads
- **HTTPS Everywhere** v2014.4.25 — Automatically use HTTPS on supported sites
- **Ad Block** v2.7.3 — Block ads across the web
- **Wappalyzer** v2.32 — Identifies software on websites
- **Force Custom Fonts** v2.2.1.0 — Force custom fonts on web pages
- **LastPass: Free Password Manager** v3.1.33 — Password manager
- **Keep My Opt-Outs** v1.0.15 — Opt out of ad personalization cookies
- **Terms of Service; Didn't Read** v1.0.7 — Instant info about ToS and privacy
  policies
- **Kill News Feed** v1.0.7 — Remove Facebook news feed
- **Auto Reloader** v1.3.0 — Auto-refresh pages after errors
- **HTML5ify** v0.5 — Force HTML5 media instead of Flash
- **Reddit Enhancement Suite** v4.3.2.1 — Reddit enhancements
- **Bug Me Not Lite** v0.3.10 — Bypass web registration via bugmenot.com
- **Tabulator** v0.0.0.1 — Linked Data Browser
- **Domain Icons** v0.0.1 — Icons next to Google search results
- **Reload All Tabs** v3.2.1 — Reload all tabs (Ctrl+Shift+R)
- **Save to Pocket** v1.8.3 — Save articles and videos
- **RSS Subscription Extension** (by Google) v2.2.3 — One-click RSS subscription
- **Auto Scroll** v3.8 — Customizable autoscroll
- **Screen Capture** (by Google) v5.1.4 — Capture page content as PNG
- **Google Drive** v6.3 — Create and share documents

Most are available for Firefox. Thanks to [Share Extensions for Google
