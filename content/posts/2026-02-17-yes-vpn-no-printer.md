> A monk asked the master: "Why can I not reach the printer?"
>
> The master replied: "Because you are still connected to the mountain."
>
> The monk disconnected from the VPN. The document printed itself.

**Note to self**: before attempting to print out a document via a Wi-Fi
connected printer, remember to disconnect from the office VPN.
