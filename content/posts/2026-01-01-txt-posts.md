[Terence Eden](https://shkspr.mobi/blog/2025/12/a-small-collection-of-text-only-websites/):

> A couple of years ago, I started serving my blog posts as plain text. Add .txt
> to the end of any URl and get a deliciously lo-fi, UTF-8, mono[chrome|space]
> alternative.

I like the idea! Using [Hugo](https://gohugo.io/) it's quite
straightforward to do so[^1]:

```diff
% git --no-pager show 590bb2751
```
