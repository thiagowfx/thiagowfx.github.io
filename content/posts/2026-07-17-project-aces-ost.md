[Project Aces](https://acecombat.fandom.com/wiki/Project_Aces) (from the [Ace
Combat](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ace_Combat) series) has one of the best
videogame [OSTs](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Soundtrack) ever.

I am[^1] finally pleased[^2] to find their whole collection available on
[Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/artist/1dhKyn9jcCoyrxGq4tI7Vc) as of 2026.
Previously, only a handful of them were available.

No more fiddling random playlists on [YouTube
Music](https://music.youtube.com/)!

See also: [radio]({{< ref "/radio" >}}).
