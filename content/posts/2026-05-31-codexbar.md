[Peter Steinberger's](https://github.com/steipete) excellent [`codexbar`](https://codexbar.app/):

> CodexBar tracks usage windows, credit balances, and reset countdowns across
> the providers you actually pay for — one status item each, or merge them into
> one.

It's a tiny app that sits in the macOS system tray.

Every imaginable provider is supported. Notable examples include: Codex
(OpenAI), Claude Code (Anthropic), OpenCode.
